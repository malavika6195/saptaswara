Our Courses

At Saptaswara Academy, we offer structured Carnatic music classes for students aged 10 and above. Choose the level that suits your experience and goals:

Beginner Level

₹500 per month
  • Introduction to Carnatic music basics
  • Voice culture and breathing techniques
  • Basic swaras and simple geethams
  • Fundamental rhythm patterns
  • 2 classes per week (45 minutes each)

Ideal for: Complete beginners with no prior musical training

Enroll Now

Intermediate Level

₹750 per month
  • Advanced voice modulation techniques
  • Varnams and simple kritis
  • Introduction to ragam basics
  • More complex tala patterns
  • 2 classes per week (60 minutes each)

Ideal for: Students with basic knowledge of Carnatic music fundamentals

Enroll Now

Advanced Level

₹1000 per month
  • Complex kritis and ragam elaboration
  • Manodharma sangeetham (improvisation)
  • Advanced techniques like neraval and kalpanaswaram
  • Concert preparation and performance skills
  • 3 classes per week (60 minutes each)

Ideal for: Experienced students looking to master advanced concepts

About Me

Malavika Sreesandesh

Carnatic Music Teacher

Namaste! I'm Malavika Sreesandesh, a passionate Carnatic music teacher with 15 years of dedicated training and practice in this ancient art form.

I have completed my diploma from the prestigious Annamalai School of Music, where I honed my skills under the guidance of renowned masters in the field.

My journey with Carnatic music began at a young age, and over the years, I have developed a deep understanding of its intricate techniques, rich traditions, and spiritual essence.

Through Saptaswara Academy, I aim to share my knowledge and love for Carnatic music with students of all ages (10 years and above) and backgrounds, helping them discover the joy and discipline of this beautiful classical tradition.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to advance your existing skills, I'm committed to providing personalized guidance to help you achieve your musical goals.

My Teaching Philosophy

Passion

I believe in nurturing a genuine love for music in every student.

Balance

Balancing technical precision with emotional expression is key to mastering Carnatic music.

Personalized Approach

Each student is unique, and I tailor my teaching methods to individual learning styles.
